Ville Oké is a Stockholm based graphic designer specialized in brand identity systems that work seamlessly across printed and digital media.

Since 2009 he has worked for some of Swedens most renowned design agencies, with clients ranging from giants such as Coca-Cola, Volvo and IKEA to smaller ones such as Valbo Shopping Mall and gambling helpline; Stödlinjen.

In 2016, Ville founded Oké Design with the vision of using intelligent imagination, a method of combining rationality and creativity, as a tool to help clients create impact. By collaborating with developers, writers and photographers, Oké Design has all the capabilites of a large design agency, but with the efficiency and flexibility of a one-man-studio.

Feel free to get in contact to see how intelligent imagination might help your business.
